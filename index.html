Getting here

Travel

Flying

The best way to get to Sibiu International Airport (SBZ) from Central, South and Western Europe (or anywhere else in the world) is flying with a layover in Germany (Munich) or Austria (Vienna). Other options include also flying to Cluj International Airport (CLJ) or to Bucharest Otopeni Airport (OTP) and renting a car to get to Sibiu.


  Zurich - Sibiu (SBZ) (with layover)

  Geneva - Sibiu (SBZ) (with layover)

  Memmingen (Germany) - Sibiu (SBZ) (direct)

  Basel - Cluj-Napoca (CLJ) (direct) +   ~2 hours to Sibiu.


If flying to Bucharest (OTP) is the better option, one can get to Sibiu either by driving (recommended) or by train:


  Bucuresti - Sibiu (~4 hours)

  Bucuresti - Sibiu


Adjust the dates and times above as they best suit your needs.

Brasov

Nestled against the backdrop of the Southern Carpathian Mountains, Brasov enchants visitors with its captivating blend of gothic, baroque, and renaissance architecture, earning its place as one of Romania's most picturesque and sought-after destinations.
